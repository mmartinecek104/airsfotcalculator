Understanding Airsoft Ballistics

Airsoft performance is dictated by kinetic energy, measured in Joules. The formula for calculation is $E_k = \frac{1}{2}mv^2$. MatiDev Calc provides an accurate conversion between FPS (feet per second) and Joules based on the weight of the BB.

Why Joules matter: Velocity alone doesn't tell the whole story. A heavier BB at the same FPS carries significantly more energy, which is why most professional airsoft fields use Joule limits for safety.

Effective Range: Our algorithm estimates effective range based on energy output and standard hop-up performance across various platforms like AEG, GBB, and Sniper systems.
